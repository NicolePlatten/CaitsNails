Our Services

From classic treatments to custom creations, every service is performed with high-quality products and strict hygiene standards.

Manicures

Classic, luxury spa, and gel manicures. Includes nail shaping, cuticle care, hydration treatment, and long-lasting high-shine finish.

Custom Nail Art

Minimalist designs, hand-painted art, glitter, French tips, 3D accents, and seasonal styles — tailored exactly to your vision.

Nail Extensions

Lightweight, durable gel and acrylic extensions. Shaped and styled to give you natural-looking strength and length.

Pedicures

Relaxing foot soaks, exfoliation, callus treatment, deep massage, and finish with your choice of regular or gel polish.

Nail Repair & Care

Fix broken or damaged nails, treat weak/brittle nails, and get professional advice to keep your natural nails strong and healthy.

Maintenance & Refills

Keep your set looking fresh. Refills, reshaping, colour changes, and repairs done quickly and professionally.

About Caits Lab

Located in Birmingham, Caits Lab is built on a passion for nail art and a commitment to high standards. We believe your nails are an extension of your personal style — and they deserve the best possible care.

Every product we use is carefully selected for safety, quality, and durability. We follow strict hygiene and sanitisation protocols so you can relax and enjoy your experience completely.

Whether you want something subtle for everyday wear or bold designs for a special occasion, we work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.
